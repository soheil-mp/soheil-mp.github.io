The following are the exact steps to ensure that you will reach your goal, according to the book Think and Grow Rich:

  1. Desire: Ignite a burning desire and obsession toward your goal (not hope or wish). Write a statement with the following information and read it twice a day:
    1. Specify the desired amount of money (very exact).
    1. Determine what you intend to give in return for that money.
    1. Set a deadline.
    1. Create a plan.
  1. Faith: Faith is the complete trust or confidence in something (which comes from your subconscious mind). Faith can be induced through auto-suggestion.
  1. Auto-Suggestion: Auto-suggestion is the subconscious adoption of an idea (through the five senses). The procedure of auto-suggestion is as follows:
    • Repeat a statement (e.g., "Day by day, in every way, I am getting better and better.")
    • Mix it with emotion and faith. Note: Plain, unemotional words do not influence the subconscious mind.
  1. Knowledge: Knowledge is potential power. It is real power if it's mixed with lots of actions and it directs you toward your goal. The requirements for getting knowledge are as follows:
    • Know where to get it.
    • Take action using that knowledge.
    • Make sure it directs you toward your goal.
  1. Imagination: Imagination is a faculty that can create and shape your reality. Man can create anything that he can imagine.
  1. Plan: Everything man creates or acquires begins in the form of planned actions. The cycle of planning is as follows:
    1. Create a plan.
    1. Take massive action.
    1. Re-adjust your plan in case of failure (i.e., temporary defeat).
    1. Go back to step 2 until you win.
  1. Decision: Decision (opposite of procrastination) is the act of deciding something when the moment has come. The criteria for making a decision are as follows:
    • Be courageous.
    • Assume risk.
    • Have faith.
  1. Persistence: Persistence is the action of doing something continuously (no matter if it's hard or is opposed by people). Persistence is the direct result of habit. This is the main insurance against failure. How to develop persistence:
    • Take action until it becomes a fixed habit (by using your will-power).
    • Create a friendly group of alliance to create stronger harmony toward your goal.
  1. Master Mind: A mastermind is a person who supplies the directing or creative intelligence for a project.
  1. Sex Transmutation: Most actions are taken dominantly by emotions (rather than cold reasons). The strongest emotion is the desire for sexual expression. Men with the highest sex energy are the most efficient salesmen. Avoid stimulation in excess, such as alcoholism and narcotics.
  1. Subconscious Mind ("Sending Station"): The subconscious mind is a data-bank of everything that is outside of our conscious awareness. It records and files everything (through thoughts, conversations, our five senses, etc.). It's a link between the finite mind and Infinite Intelligence. The subconscious mind is the "sending station" of the brain, which vibrations of thought are broadcast. The subconscious mind cannot be controlled, but you can voluntarily hand over to it any plan, desire, or purpose you want to have.
  1. Brain ("Receiving Station"): One of the brain's responsibilities is to act as a receiving station and to pick up thoughts from the ether.
  1. Sixth Sense: The sixth sense is when we have contact between the finite mind and an infinite intelligence. Listen to your sixth sense.
  1. Outwit Your Fears: Fears are just a state of mind, and it can be controlled and directed. Eliminate the fear by:
    • Reach the decision of accepting your fear.
    • Recognize your fear as a great blessing (and not as a handicap).
    • Keep your mind busy with a definite purpose, backed by a definite plan.
